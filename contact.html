Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a custom order for traditional fabrics?
We specialize in custom orders! Contact us through the form above or call our support line. Provide details about the fabric type, design, measurements, and deadline. Our team will guide you through the process and provide a quote.
What is your return policy for fabrics?
Due to the custom nature of our fabrics, returns are accepted within 14 days only for unused fabrics in original condition. Custom orders are non-returnable. Please contact us for a return authorization before sending anything back.
Do you delivered to all states?
Yes, we delivered to all state! International orders typically take 7-14 business days. Shipping costs vary by destination and order weight. We handle all customs documentation for a smooth delivery process.
How can I verify the authenticity of your fabrics?
All our fabrics come with a Certificate of Authenticity. We work directly with traditional weaving communities and provide detailed information about the origin and weaving techniques. You can also request a sample before purchasing.
Do you offer wholesale prices for bulk orders?
Absolutely! We offer special pricing for designers, boutiques, and event planners. Contact us now for a customized quote based on your quantity and fabric requirements.
