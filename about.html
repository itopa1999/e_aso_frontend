About Our Marketplace

Preserving tradition, empowering artisans, and bringing the beauty of authentic Nigerian textiles to the world.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Aso Oke & Aso Ofi Marketplace began as a passion project to preserve and promote Nigeria's rich textile heritage. Our founders, Aminat Yusuf and Chinedu Okoro, noticed that traditional weavers were struggling to reach modern markets while preserving their ancestral techniques.

What started as a small initiative connecting local artisans with urban customers has grown into Nigeria's premier online marketplace for authentic handwoven textiles. We've connected over 500 master weavers with customers in more than 30 countries, helping to sustain traditional craftsmanship in the digital age.

Today, we're proud to support artisan communities across Nigeria, providing fair compensation for their exceptional skills while bringing the beauty of Aso Oke and Aso Ofi to a global audience. Every fabric sold on our platform tells a story of cultural heritage and skilled craftsmanship.
